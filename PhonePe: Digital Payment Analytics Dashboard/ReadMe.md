# PhonePe Digital Payment Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

The PhonePe Digital Payment Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project developed to analyze digital payment transactions across multiple services offered by PhonePe, including Insurance, Loans, Money Transfer, and Recharge & Bill Payments.

The primary objective of this project is to monitor transaction performance, analyze successful and failed payments, identify the major reasons behind payment failures, and understand transaction behavior across different services. The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful business insights through interactive and visually structured reporting.

This project was designed to provide a centralized analytical view of digital payment activities, service-wise transaction trends, payment performance, and failed transaction patterns using interactive dashboards and business-focused visual storytelling.

---

# Business Problem

Digital payment platforms process a high volume of transactions every day, making payment monitoring and failure analysis an important business requirement. Failed transactions can negatively impact customer experience, reduce platform trust, and affect overall service performance.

Manually analyzing large transactional datasets can be difficult and time-consuming. This project helps simplify digital payment analysis by providing a centralized and interactive dashboard that improves visibility into transaction trends, payment failures, and service-wise performance through structured business reporting.

The dashboard supports better understanding of digital payment behavior and helps identify operational patterns affecting transaction success and failure rates.

# Dashboard Pages

## 1. Home Page

The Home Page provides a centralized overview of overall digital payment performance and transaction activity across all services. This section was designed to give a quick understanding of total transactions, successful and failed payments, transaction trends, and overall service performance through high-level KPI reporting and summary visualizations. It simplifies large transactional datasets into an easy-to-understand business overview for monitoring overall platform performance.

---

## 2. Insurance Analysis

The Insurance Analysis page focuses on analyzing payment transactions related to different insurance services. This section helps evaluate payment trends, transaction performance, and failed payment behavior across insurance categories while improving visibility into service-wise insurance transaction activities through interactive reporting and visual analysis.

---

## 3. Loans Analysis

The Loans Analysis page was designed to analyze loan-related payment transactions and transaction performance across different loan services. This section helps identify successful and failed payment patterns, monitor loan transaction trends, and evaluate service-wise loan payment behavior through structured visual reporting.

---

## 4. Money Transfer Analysis

The Money Transfer Analysis page focuses on evaluating transaction activities across different money transfer methods and payment channels. This section helps analyze transfer trends, monitor transaction performance, and identify failed payment behavior to improve visibility into digital transfer activities and user transaction patterns.

---

## 5. Recharge & Bills Analysis

The Recharge & Bills Analysis page analyzes recharge and bill payment transactions across multiple utility and recharge services. This section helps evaluate transaction trends, monitor payment performance, and identify failed transaction patterns while improving visibility into service-wise recharge and bill payment behavior through interactive reporting.
